Who We Are

About Fortune Tiles & Pavers

FORTUNE TILES AND PAVERS is a VADODARA-based market-leading company, successfully offering flooring solutions for exteriors for over 11 years.

We manufacture a wide and exclusive range of cement-based flooring products using state-of-the-art Apollo Zenith German technology machinery — ensuring best quality and range of materials available in volumes as required.

We have established an excellent track record in Quality and Services, consistently meeting customer satisfaction while offering great products at competitive prices — never compromising on quality.

  • Value system committed to giving the best to customers
  • Quality (Conformance and performance) assured products
  • Finest infrastructure to cater to bulk requirements in short times
  • Widest range of products, colours, textures, etc.

Infrastructure

Land over 60,000 sq.ft with built-up space exceeding 5,000 sq.ft. Fully automatic block-making plants with automated material handling.

Own Fleet

Company-owned transportation vehicles ensuring reliable delivery and product integrity to your site — handling delivery emergencies with ease.

Quality Standards

Stage-wise inspection & testing per IS standards. Top-grade raw materials with vendor approval and periodic vendor rating & review.

German Technology

Apollo Zenith machinery from Germany — hydraulic press, vibrator machines and varied manufacturing lines for the widest product range.
