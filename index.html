Manufacturing Process

How We Make Our Pavers

Two distinct techniques — each engineered for a different purpose. Understanding the process helps you choose the right paver for your project.

Technology

Vibro Compaction Pavers

Dense · Strong · Built for heavy loads

Steel Mould Material
High Output Volume

Dry cement mix is compacted at high frequency using Apollo Zenith German designed machinery. The intense vibration eliminates every air void — producing pavers of exceptional density and load-bearing capacity.

Extremely high compressive strength
Consistent texture & colour throughout
High volume — suits large projects
Long service life under heavy loads
5-Step Manufacturing Process
01
Raw Material Preparation

Cement, coarse aggregates, mineral pigments and minimal water blended to a dry, stiff consistency for maximum compaction efficiency.

02
Steel Mould Filling

Heavy-duty steel moulds filled with precisely measured quantities of dry mix to ensure dimensional consistency across every batch.

03
High-Frequency Vibro Compaction

Apollo Zenith machine applies intense high-frequency vibration — compacting under pressure and eliminating every internal air pocket.

04
Instant Demoulding

Compaction gives fresh pavers enough green strength to hold shape. Immediately released from steel moulds and stacked on pallets.

05
28-Day Water Curing

Extended water curing develops full compressive strength and long-term durability — meeting IS standards for load-bearing applications.

Best suited for
Roads & Highways Driveways Industrial Floors Public Plazas Parking Areas
Technique

Rubbermould Pavers

Refined · Decorative · Precisely shaped

Smooth Surface Finish
Rubber Mould Material

Cement mix is cast into flexible rubber moulds available in a wide variety of decorative shapes. Rubber's natural flexibility peels cleanly from set concrete — delivering sharp edges and smooth surfaces impossible to achieve with rigid steel moulds.

Sharp edges & smooth finish
Wide variety of decorative shapes
Rich colour retention with pigments
Perfect for landscaping & aesthetics
5-Step Manufacturing Process
01
Premium Mix Preparation

Cement, fine aggregates and iron oxide colour pigments carefully blended to a workable, pourable consistency for clean mould filling.

02
Rubber Mould Casting

Mix poured into flexible rubber moulds — available in a wide variety of shapes from classic brick patterns to ornate decorative designs.

03
Mould Vibrator Settling

Gentle vibration on a mould vibrator table removes air bubbles and ensures the mix fills every corner and intricate edge perfectly.

04
24-Hour Initial Setting

Moulds left undisturbed for 24 hours, allowing concrete to achieve initial set and sufficient green strength before demoulding.

05
Flexible Demould & 28-Day Curing

Rubber flexibility peels cleanly from set concrete, preserving crisp edges and fine surface detail. Water cured for full strength.

Best suited for
Garden Pathways Courtyards Landscaping Decorative Areas Outdoor Flooring
